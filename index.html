📅 Your Week-by-Week Experience

We've designed this journey to be efficient, supportive, and results-driven.

Here's exactly what happens each week—and what you can expect from us. 💪

Week 0: Readiness 📋

PREPARATION PHASE

What Happens First — Your Prep Work:

  • ✓ Assemble your TACT Team: Sponsor + Super User + 2 SMEs + 3-5 Change Agents (All members must complete Jules Learning Program)
  • ✓ Complete mandatory reading (15-30 min): Workflow integrity, Jules best practices, change management fundamentals
  • ✓ Host internal working session: Collaboratively answer all DEFINE & DESIGN questions
  • ✓ Prepare 3-5 clean data files: Demonstrate what's needed and confirm AI readability
  • ✓ Begin AI Adoption Checklist: Start ADKAR framework tracking
  • ✓ Upload all work to SharePoint: Complete documentation before booking Week 1

Your TACT Team Roles:

🎯 Sponsor

Accountable for success, removes blockers, ensures progress

👤 Super User ("Agent Boss")

Owns the assistant, drives build, coordinates SMEs and Change Agents

🧠 SMEs (1-2 people)

Validate logic, test outputs, provide detailed feedback

🚀 Change Agents

Early adopters who test in real-world scenarios and lead training rollout

🔒 WEEK 1 BOOKING GATE:

All items above must be complete and visible on SharePoint before Week 1 is confirmed. Sponsor or Super User signs off on readiness.

⛔ NO EVIDENCE = NO BOOKING

Week 1: Discovery 🔍

1 HOUR

What Happens:

  • ✓ Your team presents: Problem + scope, current vs. future workflow, success metrics, edge cases
  • ✓ Confirm data readiness and workflow clarity
  • ✓ Brenda guides the discussion, asks clarifying questions, identifies gaps
  • ✓ Refine approach based on session feedback

What You Get:

🎯 Suggested Success Metrics

Recommendations for measuring efficiency, business impact, and adoption

📅 Sprint Plan Confirmed

Number of Build and Improve sessions required to deliver your solution

After Week 1 — Your Homework:

  • • Refine all DEFINE & DESIGN answers based on feedback
  • • Finalize workflow and data
  • • Complete Week 2 preparation worksheet and review house rules
  • • AI Adoption Checklist Phase 1: 75% complete (required for Week 2 booking)
  • • Remove blockers

⏰ Deadline

Complete before Week 2 Booking

🔒 WEEK 2 BOOKING GATE:

Phase 1 of AI_Adoption_Checklist_ADKAR: 75% complete and Week 2 preparation worksheet completed and shared on SharePoint.

⛔ NO EVIDENCE = NO BOOKING

Week 2: Build 🔨

1.5 HOURS

What Happens:

  • ✓ YOU drive, WE guide – Your Super User leads the build with Brenda's expert support
  • ✓ Live testing and refinement – We troubleshoot together in real-time using the 7-STEP Build methodology
  • ✓ By end of session: You have a functional AI assistant (draft version ready to test!)

What You Get:

🎉 YOUR AI ASSISTANT

A working AI solution tailored to your exact workflow and challenge

📚 Testing Tracker

AI_Testing_Tracker Excel file with step-by-step instructions for Week 3 independent testing

💪 Why This Works:

YOU own the solution from Day 1. We don't build FOR you – we build WITH you. This ensures adoption, sustainability, and team confidence.

⚠️ Important Note:

Multiple Week 2 rounds may be required for complex use cases.

Week 3: Independent Testing 🧪

INDEPENDENT WORK

What Happens:

  • ✓ TACT Team tests the assistant using real work scenarios (not practice exercises)
  • ✓ Document everything – Use AI_Testing_Tracker to capture detailed input/output examples
  • ✓ Minimum testing requirement – 5 real scenarios + 3 edge cases + 2 incorrect inputs
  • ✓ Start sharing with Change Agents – Roll out to early adopters, collect feedback
  • ✓ Continue AI_Adoption_Checklist_ADKAR progression – Work through Phase 2 items during testing period

What You Get:

🎯 VALIDATED TEST DATA

Documented results from real scenarios, edge cases, and failure modes

📊 QUALITY INSIGHTS

Clear understanding of what works, what needs adjustment, and why

🚀 WEEK 4 READINESS

Complete evidence package for Live Brain Surgery session

💡 Pro Tip:

This is YOUR week to experiment without pressure. Try different approaches, test edge cases, and make it perfect for your team. We're here if you need us, but you've got this! 💪

🔒 WEEK 4 BOOKING GATE:

Testing tracker fully completed with documented results and uploaded to SharePoint. Super User signs off and books the next session.

⛔ NO EVIDENCE = NO BOOKING

Week 4: Improve 🔧

1 HOUR

What Happens:

  • ✓ Live Brain Surgery methodology – Super User drives real-time prompt refinement based on Week 3 test results, Brenda guides
  • ✓ Confirm AI_Adoption_Checklist_ADKAR: Phase 1 at 100% completion, Phase 2 at 100% completion
  • ✓ Confirm Week 8 date – Lock in final success story session

What You Get:

🎯 Enterprise-Grade Assistant

Refined, tested, and ready for real-world usage

📈 Rollout Guidance

Strategic recommendations for broader team adoption (execution depends on your Change Management readiness)

✅ ADKAR Phase 1 & 2 Confirmed

Change management foundation validated (Phase 3 continues through Week 5-8)

🎉 This is Where the Magic Happens:

We fix what's broken using YOUR real data. No guesswork – just targeted refinement based on actual usage. By the end of this session, you have a production-ready solution! 🚀

Week 5-7: Refinement & Adoption 🚀

INDEPENDENT WORK

What Happens:

  • ✓ Continue real-world testing – Use the assistant in daily workflows
  • ✓ Monitor usage and feedback – Track adoption metrics and user satisfaction
  • ✓ Build rollout plan – Prepare strategy for broader team adoption
  • ✓ Establish feedback loop – Create channels for continuous improvement
  • ✓ Complete AI Adoption Checklist – Finish all change management tasks

What You Track:

📊 ADOPTION METRICS

  • • Number of active users
  • • Usage frequency (queries per week)
  • • Key feedback trends
  • • Quality issues identified
  • • Time/cost savings data

💡 This Phase is Critical:

Adoption doesn't happen automatically. Your TACT Team drives momentum, addresses barriers, and ensures the assistant becomes part of daily workflow. Change Agents lead training rollout and champion adoption across the team.

Turn this into your department's standard operating procedure — document the workflow, standardize usage guidelines, and embed it into onboarding materials.

Week 8: Success & Sustainability ✨

30 MINUTES

What Happens:

  • ✓ Present usage data – Share adoption metrics and ROI results
  • ✓ Quantify time saved / ROI – Document measurable business impact
  • ✓ Share lessons learned – What worked, what didn't, what you'd do differently
  • ✓ Identify scaling opportunities – Can this benefit other teams or regions?

What You Get:

🎯 SUCCESSFUL DEPLOYMENT

Validation that your solution delivers sustained value

📚 Documented Success Story

Published case study for organizational learning and scaling

🚀 Scaling Roadmap

If successful: Plan to expand to other teams, regions, or business units

💪 Your Impact Multiplies:

By Week 8, you're not just a user – you're a champion! Your success story inspires other teams, your lessons learned prevent their mistakes, and your solution becomes a template for organizational transformation. 🎯✨
